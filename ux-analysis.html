The Root Cause
Every complaint traces back to the same design flaw: the IXG app puts residents in the critical path of every access event. Visitor arrives → buzzes intercom → notification must reach resident in time → resident must respond → door release may or may not work. Five sequential failure points for what should be a 2-second interaction.

PassQR breaks this dependency entirely. Known people pre-authorize themselves via QR pass — no app notification required, no resident action needed at the door. The IXG hardware still does its job (video, QR scan, door relay). PassQR handles the identity layer. The app becomes optional again.
❌ Current IXG Model — Reactive
1. Visitor presses intercom button
2. App sends push notification (may be delayed)
3. Resident must open app in time
4. Video call must connect (~30s)
5. Door release button may fail (~50%)
✕ Visitor waits, delivery driver walks away
✅ PassQR Model — Proactive
1. Resident issues QR pass before arrival
2. Visitor receives pass in WhatsApp Wallet
3. Visitor shows QR at IXG door station
4. PassQR validates in < 2 seconds
5. IXG releases door automatically
✓ No resident interaction required
