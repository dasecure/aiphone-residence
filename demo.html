Presenter Tips
⚡ Best Flow for a 10-Minute Slot
  • Steps 1–2 (portal + scan): 2 min — set the scene
  • Step 3 (tennis booking): 2 min — show the booking flow end-to-end
  • Step 4 (visitor pass): 2 min — show WhatsApp share live
  • Step 5 (dashboard): 2 min — scan live while dashboard is on screen
  • Step 6 (wallet): 1 min — add to Wallet, close strong
📱 Two-Device Setup (Recommended)
Open the dashboard on one device and the scanner on another. When you scan a pass, the dashboard feed updates in real time — this reliably lands the "wow" moment with prospects.
🔑 Common Objections
  • No internet at the gate? — Passes are validated client-side; scan history syncs when connection returns.
  • What about elderly residents? — Management office can print QR code stickers. The system never forces digital-only.
  • Integration with existing Aiphone hardware? — The scan page runs on any phone or tablet already deployed at the intercom station.
